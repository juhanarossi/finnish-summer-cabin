As days grow longer and warmer, a new summer cabin season beckons. Before you can relax at you off-the-grid Shangri-La, put it in working order.

Finland known for its lakes and its summer cabins. There are 57,000 lakes or bodies of water which have surface area of more than one hectare or about 2.5 acres, and there were 508,918 summer cabins in Finland at the end of 2021. As the numbers suggest, owning a lakeside summer cabin, or “kesämökki” as the building is called in Finnish language, is in an integral part of Finnish lifestyle. But before we head out to a summer cabin, please take the following short and playful quiz to see whether you’re ready to leave modern comforts of city life and prepared to adapt to the summer cabin frame of mind.
